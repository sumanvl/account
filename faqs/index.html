GST questions

Who should apply for GST registration?

GST requirement depends on turnover, business type, interstate supply, ecommerce activity and notified categories.

What documents are usually needed for GST?

PAN, Aadhaar, business address proof, bank proof, photo and entity documents may be required based on applicant type.

Can GST filing support continue after registration?

Yes. Return filing, amendment, cancellation, annual return, notice reply and e-invoicing support are handled as separate service requirements.

What happens if GST clarification is issued?

The clarification must be reviewed with proper documents and submitted within the portal timeline.

Income tax questions

Which ITR form should I file?

ITR form selection depends on salary, business income, professional income, capital gains, foreign assets and entity type.

What records help with ITR filing?

Form 16, AIS, TIS, Form 26AS, bank details, investment proofs, loan details and income records should be reviewed.

Can business owners get tax planning help?

Yes. Tax planning support can review income, expenses, advance tax, deductions and compliance dates.

Can income tax notices be reviewed?

Yes. Notice replies require the notice copy, assessment year details, return records and supporting documents.

Business registration

Which registration is suitable for a new business?

Choice depends on ownership, partners, liability, funding plans, tax requirements and future compliance expectations.

Do LLP and Private Limited documents differ?

Yes. LLPs and companies need different MCA forms, partner/director details, agreements and incorporation records.

Can MSME and GST be handled together?

They are different registrations, but document preparation can be coordinated so business records remain consistent.

Is Shop and Establishment registration needed?

Applicability depends on local rules, business premises, employee count and nature of establishment.

Accounting and payroll

What is included in monthly accounting support?

It can include sales, purchase, expenses, bank reconciliation, GST data, payroll entries and financial statement preparation.

When should a business start bookkeeping?

Bookkeeping should start from the first month of activity so invoices, bank entries and expenses stay traceable.

Can payroll records include PF and ESI inputs?

Yes. Payroll support can include attendance, salary sheets, deductions, payslips and statutory inputs where applicable.

Do you prepare MIS reports?

MIS support can include revenue, expense, cash flow, receivables, payables and profit summaries for owner review.

Documents and support

Can I send documents by WhatsApp?

Initial checklist sharing can happen through WhatsApp. Sensitive records should be shared carefully and only when required.

Will I receive acknowledgement or proof?

For filing or registration work, acknowledgement, filed copy, certificate or completion note should be preserved.

How do I know the next compliance date?

Due dates depend on the service, turnover, entity type and return frequency. The relevant service page explains next steps.

Use the Documents Checklist page for GST, ITR, registration, accounting and payroll document categories.
